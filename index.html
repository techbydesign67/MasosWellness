About Us

Background & Experience

Diploma in Counselling and Communication, BSc Social Science (Honours) in Psychology (SACAP), Certified Master Life Coach, Certified Corporate Wellness Specialist, Certified Trauma Support Specialist, Specialist Wellness Counsellor (ASCHP), Relational Intelligence Practitioner, Gestalt Play Therapy, Emotional Aid.

Years of Practice

7 years supporting individuals, children, teens, couples, families, and groups.

Areas of Counselling

Personal empowerment, conflict resolution, communication skills, grief & bereavement, couples counselling, stress & anxiety management, anger management, intrapersonal and interpersonal skills development.

Counselling Philosophy & Approach

Faith-based, human-centred, insight-oriented, solution-focused, dignity-restoring, mindfulness-based. Integrative, trauma-informed, relational, strengths-based. Emotional containment, reflective dialogue, psycho-education, insight-oriented counselling.

Unique Approach

Balances emotional safety with clarity. Trauma-informed, insight-oriented, focuses on dignity, helping clients feel heard, steadier, clearer, and more confident.

Services

Counselling Services

Individual counselling, family counselling, group sessions, workshops for children, parents, teachers. Online & in-person sessions.

Workshops & Programs

Psycho-educational workshops covering interpersonal & intrapersonal skills, conflict resolution, self-esteem, emotional intelligence, resilience, multiple intelligences, parenting, social skills, emotional attunement, social-emotional learning.

Session Details

Individual sessions: R800 for 90 mins. Workshops & group programmes quoted based on needs. Resources provided include worksheets, guided exercises, reflection prompts.
